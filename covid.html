Corona virus

A coronavirus called COVID-19 has affected this world in early 2020. The infection spread globally and was declared a pandemic.

Several countries have decided to take unprecedented and severe precautions to prevent the spread of this virus.

Among these are social distancing and lock downs. These cause people to stay at home and work from home. In addition school, university and pre school closings result in children being at home.
